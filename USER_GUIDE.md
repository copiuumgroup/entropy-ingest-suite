# 🔳 Welcome to Entropy Ingest Suite!
**Your Simple Guide to Professional Audio Mastering.**

Hey! If you're new to this, don't worry. Entropy Ingest Suite is designed to make your music sound incredible with just a few clicks. Here’s how to get started.

---

## 🚀 1. Opening the App
If you are a developer, run `npm run dev` in your terminal. 
If you have a built version, just double-click **Entropy Ingest Suite.exe**.

## 📥 2. Finding Music
1.  Click on the **yt-dlp** tab (the cloud icon).
2.  Type a song name or paste a YouTube/SoundCloud link into the search bar.
3.  Click **Stage** to add it to your list.
4.  Once you have your songs, click **Run Batch Process** to download them to your computer.

## 🎛️ 3. Mastering in the Studio
This is where the magic happens! Click on the **Studio** tab.
-   **Speed**: Slow down your track for that "Slowed + Reverb" vibe.
-   **Vocal Focus**: Makes the singer's voice stand out or reduces the background music.
-   **Stereo Width**: Makes the song feel "wider" and more immersive.
-   **Saturation**: Adds a warm, professional "analog" feel to the sound.

## 💾 4. Saving Your Work
When you’re happy with how it sounds:
1.  Click the **Export** button.
2.  Choose if you want an **Audio** file (MP3/WAV) or a **Video** with cover art.
3.  Wait for the progress bar to finish. Your new masterpiece will be saved in your **Music** folder!

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting for Noobs
-   **"It says yt-dlp or ffmpeg is missing!"**: You need to install these tools on your Windows computer for the app to download music. 
-   **"The sound is glitchy"**: Make sure you don't have too many other apps open. Mastering uses a lot of computer power!
-   **"Where is my music?"**: By default, everything goes into your standard Windows **Music** folder.
